> Multi-modal pain tracking. AI pattern detection. Non-pharma intervention recommender. Opioid tapering support.

## The Solution

**PainTracker** — Intensity, location, triggers, sleep, mood, activity tracking\n**PatternDetector** — Identify personal pain triggers and effective interventions over time\n**InterventionGuide** — Evidence-based non-pharmacological options (PT, CBT, TENS, mindfulness)\n**TaperSupport** — Opioid reduction guidance with physician coordination\n**FlarePrediction** — Predict pain flares from weather, activity, stress patterns\n**CommunityBridge** — Connect to chronic pain support communities
